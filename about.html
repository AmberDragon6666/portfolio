About me

Hi, I'm Lily. I mostly go by Amberdragon online. I am a Game Design student at the University of Hull with three years of experience. I am also an artist. I have made multiple games, often working in teams of 2-6. I really like indie games, but I do play ones made by larger studios from time to time. Some of my favourites are:

  • Pathologic
  • Hollow Knight
  • Psychopomp
  • Dragon Quest
  • Pseudoregalia
  • Voices of the Void
  • Outer Wilds
  • Rain World
  • Portal
I also like music. I listen to a lot of different things depending on my mood but my favourite band by far is The Cure, especially their stuff around and before Disintegration. I read occasionally, being a fan of Anne Rice's Vampire Chronicles and the Parahumans series. My favourite films include The Good, The Bad, And The Ugly, and the Lord Of The Rings trilogy.
