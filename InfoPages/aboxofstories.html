A Box Of Stories

This was my major project in university, made in a group of two. It is an escape room game wherein the player must use clues from visual novel segments to solve puzzles in the room.

Unicorn Book

This story was written by Emily Raine, wherein a unicorn persuades a child to burn down her home. She told me the pieces that she wanted, in the style of a children's book, and I drew them.

Tape

A stolen tape shows some friends making a documentary about the case of the "Kingshelm butcher", an urban legend stemming from a series of poisonings at their namesake mall.

Dungeon

A text-based-adventure style story about someone delving into their father's tomb to avoid a deadly fate.
